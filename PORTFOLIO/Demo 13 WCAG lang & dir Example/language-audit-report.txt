Accessibility Audit Report: Multilanguage & Direction Support

Issue:
No lang or dir attributes for multilingual content, which can cause mispronunciation by screen readers or incorrect text display for RTL languages.

WCAG Standard:
WCAG 2.1 â€“ Success Criterion 3.1.1 (Language of Page), 3.1.2 (Language of Parts), 1.3.2 (Meaningful Sequence)

Impact:
Screen reader users may not understand content in other languages; RTL text may display incorrectly.

Recommendation:
Add correct lang and dir attributes for all non-default language and right-to-left content.

Result:
All multilingual and RTL content is now properly presented and accessible for all users.
